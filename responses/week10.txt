Duvall explores the evolution of graphic design through modernism, postmodernism, and "hypermodernism." It emphasizes the significance of surfaces and signs in graphic design and how these have changed from tangible, one-to-one associations to complex, interconnected symbols. Duvall also discusses how graphic design serves as a reflection of cultural shifts and technological progress  in digital world.
