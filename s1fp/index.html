Video Games

I love video games. I have been playing them for a while now and I have a number of opinions about them. I have a number of opinions about the gaming industry and games in general, but the purpose of this site is not to be a place for my rants about how DRM is a parasite on the gaming community or how all games should offer native Linux versions. This website is meant to catalogue some of my favorite games and serve as a list of games that I want to someday play. Look above to choose where to go!
